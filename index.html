Our World

“We make jewellery the slow way — one stone, one setting, one pair of hands at a time.”

Founded at a single workbench, Aela Jewels began with a simple belief: the pieces that mark our lives should be made with the same care they’ll be kept. Every design is cast from recycled gold, set with traceable stones, and finished by hand in our studio.
